Understanding Text - Announcement

Who might need this information?

Airplane passengers and people who are visiting the airport might need this information, as it is showing where the temporary location of gates and how to get to the other terminal.

Pembahasan

Announcement adalah jenis teks pengumuman yang membagian informasi mengenai suatu hal kepada banyak orang.

Announcement ada dalam bentuk tertulis maupun lisan. Untuk contoh kali ini adalah written announcement (tertulis) yang ditulis untuk memberitahukan tentang proyek renovasi bandara dan perpindahan gate juga terminal.

Generic Structure of Announcement

1. Stating Purpose: The text that contains what event will be held

2. Stating Day and Date: Day and Date realization. The text that contains when the event will be held

3. Stating Place: The text that contains where the event will be held

4. Informing Sender: The text that contains name of the person who will be contacted
