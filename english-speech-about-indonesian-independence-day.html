™Independence Day™
Independence Day is a special moment for every country that has been colonized by other nations in the past. Indonesia is one of the countries that had been colonized for a long time by the Dutch and then by Japan. Indonesia's independence on 17 August 1945. Indonesian always commemorate the independence day every 17th of August to commemorate and appreciate the services of the heroes of the nation who had struggled with the soul and body to achieve Indonesia's independence.
