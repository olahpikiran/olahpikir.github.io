15. a kilo of onion costs Rp 25.000,00 a kilo of garlic costs Rp 20.000,00 Which comparative is right?


Jawaban:

Comparative degree

A kilo of onion is more expensive than a kilo of garlic.

A kilo of garlic is cheaper than a kilo of onion

Rumus komparatif

Is + adj-er +than atauu

Is + more + adj + than

Kalau yg ditambah er itu untuk yg 1 syllable atau 1 suku kata kayak big ,cheap, small , etc

Kalau more itu untuk adjective(kt sifat) yg punya 2/lebih syllables atau suku kata contoh expensive itu 3 suku kata ex-pen-sive jadi pake more contoh lainnya beautiful , ugly , handsome ,etc

Hope it helps you (◠‿◕)

Penjelasan:

Sedikit penjelasan untuk Comparison of Degree/Kalimat Perbandingan

• Most dipakai untuk Superlative sentences yang adjectivenya lebih dari 2 suku kata

• More dipakai untuk Comparative sentences yang adjectivenya lebih dari 2 suku kata

• Tambahan Er untuk Comparative sentences

• Tambahan Est untuk Superlative sentences

29. how to make a chesee and onion sandwich​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

1. Sauté the onions: Peel the onions, halve lengthwise, then cut into cm crosswise.

2. Heat olive oil in a frying pan, add chopped onion, oregano, salt and pepper. Saute until golden brown, reduce heat, cook for 10-15 minutes until onion is soft. Lift, divide into 4 parts.

3. Heat the electric toaster / tongs. Put a piece of bread on it, place a sheet of cheese and 1 part of the sautéed onion mixture.

4. Cover a piece of bread on top, then press the lid tongs tightly. Bake for 5 – 8 minutes until cooked, open the grill, remove from heat. Finish for all remaining ingredients.

5. Put the bread on a cutting board, cut two loaves of bread into a triangular shape. Serve while warm.
