27. jika laju mesin mobil 5400 rpm,berapa nilainya dalam satuan rps​


Jawaban:

90 rps

Penjelasan:

rpm = round per minute

rps = round per second

1 minute = 60 second

1 rpm = (1/60)rps

x rps = (1/60) × y rpm

x rps = (1/60) × 5400 rpm = 90 rps
