5. berilah contohexplain expression of relief by your ownlangueexplain expression of pain by your ownlangueexplain expression of plesuare by your ownlangue


Pembahasan:

Berikut adalah contoh explanation of expression of relief, explanation of expression of pain, and explanation of expression of pleasure by my own language

explanation of expression of relief

Expression of relief is a kind of expression which is used to express someone's felling when he/she feels relief after overcoming a hard problem or ending the worry.

The example of expression of relief:

- What a relief!

- I am relieved that ...

- Thank god for ...

- That's a relief

- I'm relieved to hear that ...

Expression of pain

Expression of pain is a kind of expression which is used to express the pain that we feel. The pain may be physically or mentally hurt.

The example of expression of pain:

- It hurts

- I'm really sick

- It's really painful

- I'm so sad to hear this

- my heart is so burdened

Expression of pleasure

Expression of pleasure is a kind of expression which is used to express that someone feels glad or pleasure for having excited feeling

Examples of expression of pleasure:

- That's amazing

- How wonderful

- i like it

- I can't describe how pleased i am

- It's my pleasure

29. apa yang dimaksud dengan expression of agreement dan expression of disagreement?​


Jawaban:

Expression of Agreement/ungkapan persetujuan adalah ungkapan yang kita pakai ketika kita sependapat dengan orang lain dalam suatu hal. Misalnya teman sobat mengatakan

"I think this school needs to hold a joint istighosah every week" (“menurut saya sekolah ini perlu mengadakan acara istighosah bersama setiap minggu”). Trus kamu setuju dan mengatakan "I agree with your opinion" (“Saya setuju dengan pendapatmu")

Sedangkan Expression of Disagreement atau ungkapan ketidaksetujuan adalah ungkapan ketika kita merasa tidak sependapat dengan orang lain. Misalnya,"For this matter I do not agree with you . I agree more if istighosah is held every month".(“Untuk masalah ini saya kurang setuju dengan anda. saya lebih setuju jika istighosah diadakan setiap bulan saja”. Itulah contoh expression of disagreement.
