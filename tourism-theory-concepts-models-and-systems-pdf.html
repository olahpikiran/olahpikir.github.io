2. Define and explain supply chain management systems, supply chain simplification, and collaborative commerce, and the relationship between these concepts. !


Jawaban:

Define and explain supply chain management systems, supply chain simplification, and collaborative commerce, and the relationship between these concepts!

Supply chain management is the management of the flow of goods and services and includes all processes that transform raw materials into final products.

The examples of supply chain activities include farming, refining, design, manufacturing, packaging, and transportation.

Supply Chain Simplification means to make the series of simplifying processes in supplying products or services to people.

For example: Minimizing the distribution lines of the products.

Collaborative commerce (C-commerce) describes electronically enabled business interactions among an enterprise's internal personnel, business partners and customers throughout a trading community.

For example: All social media platforms connect to C-commerce.

The relationship between these concepts is to make each and every step of production process effective and efficient in order to give better services to customers.

Penjelasan:

Tentukan dan jelaskan sistem manajemen rantai pasokan, penyederhanaan rantai pasokan, dan perdagangan kolaboratif, dan hubungan antara konsep-konsep ini!

Manajemen rantai pasokan adalah manajemen arus barang dan jasa dan mencakup semua proses yang mengubah bahan mentah menjadi produk akhir.

Contoh aktivitas rantai pasokan termasuk pertanian, pemurnian, desain, manufaktur, pengemasan, dan transportasi.

Penyederhanaan Rantai Pasokan artinya membuat rangkaian proses penyederhanaan dalam penyediaan produk atau jasa kepada masyarakat.

Misalnya: Meminimalkan jalur distribusi produk.

Perdagangan kolaboratif (C-commerce) menggambarkan interaksi bisnis yang diaktifkan secara elektronik di antara personel internal perusahaan, mitra bisnis, dan pelanggan di seluruh komunitas perdagangan.

Misalnya: Semua platform media sosial terhubung ke C-commerce.

Hubungan antara konsep-konsep ini adalah untuk membuat setiap langkah proses produksi efektif dan efisien untuk memberikan pelayanan yang lebih baik kepada para pelanggan.

9. Pilih dan kemudian jelaskankan hanya 2 (dua) saja dari beberapa teori keputusan berikut ini: (a) Rational Theory of Choice, (b) Prospect Theory, (c) Theories of Attention and Search, (d) System Theory, (e) Bounded Rationality


Rational Theory of Choice atau 'Teori pilihan rasional dapat didefinisikan sebagai suatu kerangka pemikiran untuk memahami dan merancang model perilaku sosial dan ekonomi. Sedangkan, Prospect Theory menjelaskan mengenai bagaimana individu menilai kehilangan dan mendapatkan perspektif secara asimetris. Teori Rasionalitas Berbatas atau Bounded Rationality menjabarkan mengenai reaksi jenis investor nan lambat atau lemah. Pada saat pengumuman laba neto meningkat maka sebagian investor akan semakin waspada risiko, serta bereaksi tidak serta merta terhadap info kenaikan laba neto.

Pembahasan

Terdapat beberapa teori yang membahas mengenai perilaku-perilaku dari para pelaku kegiatan ekonomi, seperti Rational Theory of Choice, Prospect Theory, Theories of Attention and Search, System Theory, serta Bounded Rationality. Teori-teori ini membahas mengenai pola perilaku dari para pelaku ekonomi yang dapat mempengaruhi kegiatan di bidang ekonomi, seperti harga, penawaran, serta permintaan.

Jawaban:

There are six fundamental concepts of trigonometry: triangles, right triangles, angles, angle measure, trigonometric functions, and trigonometric identities.

Example 1 - Triangles: A triangle is a three-sided polygon with three angles.

Example 2 - Right Triangles: A right triangle is a triangle with one 90 degree angle.

Example 1 - Angles: An angle is formed by two lines that intersect at a given point.

Example 2 - Angle Measure: Angle measure is the measurement of an angle in terms of degrees or radians.

Example 1 - Trigonometric Functions: Trigonometric functions are mathematical equations that describe a relationship between angles and sides of a triangle.

Example 2 - Trigonometric Identities: Trigonometric identities are relationships between trigonometric functions that hold true for all angles.
