misal

u = x^3 - x^2 - 1

du = 3x^2 - 2x

dx = du/(3x^2 - 2x)

∫ (6x^2 - 4x) . u^1/2 . du/(3x^2 - 2x)

2 . ∫ . u^1/2 + C

= 2 . 1/(3/2) . u^3/2 + C

= 2 . 2/3 . u^3/2 + C
