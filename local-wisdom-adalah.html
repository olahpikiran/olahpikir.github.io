6. 1. why the essence of local wisdom is very important 2. How to apply local wisdom in Everyday life? 3. What does local wisdom mean in the modern World? 4. What are the obstaclas and solutions in implementing local wisdom?


Jawaban:

The essence of local wisdom is important because it represents the cultural and traditional values and practices of a particular community that have been passed down from generation to generation. These values and practices reflect the community's experiences and knowledge in addressing various challenges and finding solutions that are appropriate for their local context. By preserving and utilizing local wisdom, we can ensure the continuation of cultural heritage and foster sustainable development that is tailored to the needs and values of the community.To apply local wisdom in everyday life, individuals can make conscious efforts to understand and incorporate traditional values and practices into their daily routines. For example, they can learn to respect and preserve the environment, practice sustainable agriculture and fishing methods, and support community-based activities and initiatives that promote cultural heritage. Additionally, they can participate in cultural events and festivals, support local businesses and products, and engage in intergenerational dialogues that promote the exchange of knowledge and experiences.In the modern world, local wisdom is often seen as an alternative to modern and Westernized approaches to development. It is a source of inspiration for sustainable and community-based solutions that are based on the local context and cultural heritage. It also serves as a reminder of the importance of preserving cultural heritage and ensuring that traditional values and practices are not lost in the face of modernization and globalization.The main obstacles in implementing local wisdom include the lack of recognition and support from the government, the influence of Western-style development, and the loss of traditional knowledge and practices due to the aging population and modernization. To overcome these obstacles, it is important to raise awareness about the significance of local wisdom and promote its integration into development programs and policies. Additionally, efforts must be made to preserve and revitalize traditional knowledge and practices, and support intergenerational dialogues that encourage the exchange of knowledge and experiences.

16. synonymdiscrimination=recognize=determine=defense=wisdom=​


Synonym = Persamaan makna kata

1. discrimination (diskriminasi/sifat memilih-milih/membeda-bedakan)

= distinction, classification, diversification (pembedaan)

= difference, discrepancy, diversity, divergence (perbedaan)

= separation, segregation, splitting, isolation, disconnection (pemisahan)

2. recognize (mengakui)

= admit, acknowledge, confess, concede, profess (mengakui)

= identify, spot (mengenali)

= realize, be aware, awake, sense (sadar)

= appreciate, respect, value, cherish, reward (menghargai)

= legitimate, legalize (memperakui)

3. determine (menentukan)

= specify, decide, set, establish, assign (menentukan)

= stipulate, define (menetapkan)

= ensure, confirm, assure, insure (memastikan)

= break, disconnect, sever, choose (memutuskan)

= strengthen, stabilize, introduce (mempertetapkan)

4. defense (pertahanan)

= defense, fortress, bukwark, entrenchment, vindication, breastwork (pertahanan)

= advocacy, plea, pleading, protection, buckler (pembelaan)

= guard, custody, preservation, surveillance (penjagaan)

5. wisdom (kebijaksanaan)

= discretion, tact, prudence, expediency (kebijaksanaan)

= discernment, ability (kearifan)

= subtlety (kebijakan)

wisdom (kebijaksanaan) = caution (berhati - hati)

Penjelasan:

Untuk jawaban pertama :

Determine, contoh kalimat : He is going to determine that we will get promotion or not (Dia akan memutuskan bahwa kami akan mendapat promosi atau tidak)

Decide, contoh kalimat : We decided to go to our grandma's house this weekend (kami memutuskan untuk pergi ke rumah nenek akhir minggu ini)

Untuk soal kedua :

Defense : The king maximizes the defense of the castle because enemy is coming to their castle. (Sang raja memaksimalkan pertahanan kerajaan karena musuh sedang menuju istana.)

Guard : The guard of that famous singer is watching around while the singer is jogging (Pengawal penyanyi terkenal itu sedang mengawasi sekitar ketika penyanyi itu sedang joging)

Untuk soal ketiga :  

Wisdom : The soft sheen, the superb gilding. Not to mention the centuries of wisdom they contain.(Kilau lembutnya, kemasan terbaiknya, belum lagi kearifan abad yang dikandungnya.

Caution : Cleaning service put the wet floor caution after he mops the floor. (Petugas kebersihan menaruh peringatan lantai basah setelah dia mengepel lantai.)

Untuk soal keempat :

Konsep Tri Hita Karana yang diterapkan di Bali dengan makna manusia harus menjaga keharmonisan hubungan antara manusia dengan sesama manusia, manusia dengan Tuhan, danmanusia dengan lingkungan alam, merupakan perwujudan dari..(A) local advisory(B)local possibilism(C)local wisdom(D)local fairytale(E)local determinism​


Konsep Tri Hita Karana yang diterapkan di Bali dengan makna manusia harus menjaga keharmonisan hubungan antara manusia dengan sesama manusia, manusia dengan Tuhan, dan manusia dengan lingkungan alam, merupakan perwujudan dari:

(C) Local wisdom

Pembahasan

Local wisdom atau dalam bahasa Indonesia disebut Kearifan Lokal adalah nilai dan norma yang ada pada suatu tatanan masyarakat yang dijadikan pedoman dalam bertindak dan berperilaku. Kearifan lokal bersifat lokal dan biasanya hanya diakui oleh masyarakat yang bersangkutan.

Konsep Tri Hita Karana merupakan contoh kearifan lokal masyarakat Bali.  Jika kita lihat asal katanya, bisa disimpulkan:

→ Tri, berarti tiga.

→ Hita, berarti kebahagiaan.

→ Karana, berarti penyebab.

Tri Hita Karana berarti “Tiga penyebab kebahagiaan”.

Nilai kearifan lokal Tri Hita Karana mengajarkan kita bahwa untuk bisa memperoleh kebahagiaan dalam hidup, kita harus menjaga hubungan baik secara vertikal (dengan Tuhan) dan secara horizontal (dengan sesama manusia dan alam).

Kesimpulan umum yang dapat ditarik dari buku tersebut adalah bahwa untuk mencapai kebebasan, seseorang perlu belajar dan mengerti akan kebijaksanaan. Kebebasan tidak hanya diukur dari fisik, tetapi juga dari pikiran dan emosi. Oleh karena itu, untuk mencapai kebebasan yang sebenarnya, seseorang harus bebas dari batasan-batasan yang ditetapkan oleh diri sendiri dan lingkungannya. Ini dapat dicapai melalui proses belajar dan pengembangan diri.
