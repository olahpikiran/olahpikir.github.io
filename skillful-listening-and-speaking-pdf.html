1. What are differences between imitative speaking and imitative writing, intensive reading and intensive speaking, and responsive listening and extensive speaking?


Imitative speaking is simply the ability to parrot back a word or phrase or a sentence.
Examples include directed response tasks, reading aloud, sentence and dialogue completion, limited picture-cued tasks.

Imitative writing requires students to demonstrate skills in the fundamental tasks of writing letters, correctly spelling words, correctly placing punctuation marks, and constructing very brief sentences

Intensive reading is focuses on closely following a shorter text, doing exercises with it, and learning it in detail. According to this approach, this helps language learners really understand the language’s grammar and syntax.

Intensive speaking involves producing a limit amount of language in a highly control context. An example of this would be to read aloud a passage or give a direct response to a simple question.

Responsive listening involves listening to a small amount of a language such as a command, question, or greeting. After listening, the student is expected to develop an appropriate short response.

Extensive speaking is normal some sort of monolog.
Examples include speech, story-telling, etc. This involves a great deal of preparation and is not typically improvisational communication.

14. 3. Change the verbs to Simple Present and Present Continuous!Present ContinuousSimple Present(VERB+ING)SpeakSpeakingListeningStudy...WriteS..RidingRun​


Jawaban:

Present Continuous Tense

Membentuk present continuous

Present continuous dari kata kerja apa pun terdiri dari dua bagian: present tense dari kata kerja to be + present participle dari kata kerja utama.

(Bentuk present participle adalah: kata dasar + ing, seperti talking, playing, moving, smiling)

Positif

Subjek + to be + kata dasar + ing

She is talking.

Negatif

Subjek + to be + not + kata dasar + ing

She is not (isn't) talking

Pertanyaan

to be + subjek + kata dasar + ing

Is she talking?

Contoh: TO GO, present continuous

Positif Negatif Pertanyaan

I am going. I am not going. Am I going?

You are going. You aren't going. Are you going?

He, she, it is going. He, she, it isn't going. Is he, she, it going?

We are going. We aren't going. Are we going?

You are going. You aren't going. Are you going?

They are going. They aren't going. Are they going?

Catatan: singkatan (contraction) negatif alternatif: I'm not going, you're not going, he's not going, dsb.

Fungsi dari present continuous

Sama seperti semua tense dalam bahasa Inggris, sikap penutur sama pentingnya dengan waktu kejadian atau tindakan. Saat seseorang menggunakan present continuous, mereka sedang memikirkan sesuatu yang belum selesai atau belum berakhir.

Present continuous digunakan:

untuk mendeskripsikan tindakan yang sedang terjadi saat ini: You are using the Internet. You are studying English grammar.

untuk mendeskripsikan tindakan yang sedang terjadi dalam suatu periode waktu atau tren: Are you still working for the same company? More and more people are becoming vegetarian.

untuk mendeskripsikan tindakan atau kejadian di masa depan yang sudah direncanakan atau disiapkan: We're going on holiday tomorrow. I'm meeting my boyfriend tonight. Are they visiting you next winter?

untuk mendeskripsikan kejadian atau situasi sementara: He usually plays the drums, but he's playing bass guitar tonight. The weather forecast was good, but it's raining at the moment.

jika digunakan dengan always, forever, constantly, untuk mendeskripsikan dan memberi penekanan pada serangkaian tindakan berulang yang masih berlanjut: Harry and Sally are always arguing! You're constantly complaining about your mother-in-law!

Penjelasan:

'Talking' merupakan kata yang digunakan berunsuran seperti "chatting" (membicarakan). Sedangkan 'speaking' memiliki unsur (bicaralah/bicara) seperti 'speak up' (bicaralah!)

Contoh:

1. What are you guys talking about?

- We're talking about our school homework today.

Kita sedang membicarakan tentang tugas sekolah hari ini.

2. What language are you speaking?

- I'm speaking Indonesian language.

Saya bicara menggunakan bahasa Indonesia.

'Hearing' memiliki arti 'dengar'. Dan 'listening' memiliki arti 'mendengarkan'

Contoh:

1. What? Can you repeat? I don't hear you just now.

2. What song are you listening to?

- I'm listening to 'Money'.

Memiliki arti yang sama namun berbeda. Tergantung kalimat.
