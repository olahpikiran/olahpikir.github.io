12. Buatlah 10 kalimat present continous tense contohnya seperti di bawah ini(+) my father is reading a newspaper(-) my father isn't reading a newspaper(?) Is my father reading a newspaper?


1.

(+) I am making a glass of ice tea. / Aku sedang membuat segelas es teh.

(-) I am not making a glass of ice tea. / Aku tidak sedang membuat segelas es teh.

(?) Are you making a glass of ice tea? / Apakah kamu sedang membuat segelas es teh?

2.

(+) My brother is studying in his room. / Kakakku sedang belajar di kamarnya.

(-) My brother is not studying in his room. / Kakakku tidak sedang belajar di kamarnya.

(?) Is your brother studying in his room? / Apakah kakakmu sedang belajar di kamarnya?

3.

(+) My mother is ironing the clothes right now. / Ibuku sedang menyetrika pakaian sekarang.

(-) My mother is not ironing the clothes right now. / Ibuku tidak sedang menyetrika pakaian sekarang.

(?) Is your mother ironing the clothes right now? / Apakah ibumu sedang menyetrika pakaian sekarang?

4.

(+) My father is watching the TV at the moment. / Ayahku sedang menonton TV sekarang.

(-) My father is not watching the TV at the moment. / Ayahku tidak sedang menonton TV sekarang.

(?) Is your father watching the TV at the moment? / Apakah ayahmu sedang menonton TV sekarang?

5.

(+) I and my friends are doing the homework. / Aku dan teman-temanku sedang mengerjakan PR.

(-) I and my friends are not doing the homework. / Aku dan teman-temanku tidak sedang mengerjakan PR?

(?) Are they doing the homework? / Apakah mereka sedang mengerjakan PR?

6.

(+) Tina is staying at her aunt’s house this week. / Tina sedang tinggal di rumah bibinya minggu ini.

(-) Tina is not staying at her aunt’s house this week. / Tina tidak sedang tinggal di rumah bibinya minggu ini.

(?) Is Tina staying at her aunt’s house this week? / Apakah Tina sedang tinggal di rumah bibinya minggu ini?

7.

(+) My sister is sleeping in my bedroom. / Adikku sedang tidur di kamarku.

(-) My sister is not sleeping in my bedroom. / Adikku tidak sedang tidur di kamarku.

(?) Is your sister sleeping in your bedroom? / Apakah adikmu sedang tidur di kamarmu?

8.

(+) Bobby is preparing his presentation. / Bobby sedang menyiapkan presentasinya.

(-) Bobby is not preparing his presentation. / Bobby tidak sedang menyiapkan presentasinya.

(?) Is Bobby preparing his presentation? / Apakah Bobby sedang menyiapkan presentasinya?

9.

(+) My nephew is eating porridge on his high chair. / Keponakanku sedang makan bubur di kursi tingginya.

(-) My nephew is not eating porridge on his high chair. / Keponakanku tidak sedang makan bubur di kursi tingginya.

(?) Is your nephew eating porridge on his high chair? / Apakah keponakanmu sedang makan bubur di kursi tingginya?

10.

(+) My mother is taking a nap. / Ibuku sedang tidur siang.

(-) My mother is not taking a nap. / Ibuku tidak sedang tidur siang.

(?) Is your mother taking a nap? / Apakah ibumu sedang tidur siang?

19. Apa itu reading for detailed information dan berikan contohnya dalam kehidupan sehari-hari?


Reading for detailed information adalah membaca sebuah informasi secara rinci. Dalam bahasa Inggris, hal itu memiliki istilah tersendiri yaitu reading comprehension. Contohnya adalah membaca berbagai sumber untuk referensi penulisan tugas akhir, skripsi atau artikel ilmiah lainnya.

29. contoh soal reading comprehensim pilihan ganda beserta jawabannya


Questions 1-10 refer to the following passage.

The most familiar speleothems (from the Greek word spelaion for the cave and thema for deposit), the decorative dripstone features found in caves, are stalactites and stalagmites. Stalactites hang downward from the ceiling of the cave and are formed as drop after drop of water slowly trickles through crack on the cave roof. Stalagmites grow upward from the floor of the cave, generally as a result of water dripping from an overhead stalactite. A column forms when a stalactite and a stalagmite grow until they join. A “curtain” or “drapery” begins to form on an inclined ceiling when drops of water trickle along a slope.

Natural openings on the surface that lead to caves are called sinkholes. or swallow holes. Streams sometimes disappear down these holes and flow through the cavern. Rivers may flow from one mountain to another through a series of caves . Some caverns have sinkholes in their floors. Water often builds up a rim of dripstone around the edge of the hole. Dripping water often contains dissolved minerals as well as acid. These minerals too will be deposited; and they may give rich coloring to the deposits. If minerals in the water change, layers of different colors may be formed.

1.Stalagmites are formed by …

(A) drops of water which enter through cracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

2. Sinkholes are …

(A) the decorative dripstone features found in caves.

(B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

(C) colorful layers of mineral deposits.

(D) None of the above

Jawaban : (B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

3. Which speleothem grows upward from the floor?

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Sinkholes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (A) Stalagmites

4. An “inclined ceiling” is one which …

(A) is straight.

(B) is crooked.

(C) is slanted.

(D) is wet.

Jawaban : (C) is slanted.

5. Which of the following are NOT caused by dripping water?

(A) Stalactites

(B) Stalagmites

(C) Slopes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (C) Slopes

6. The information in the passage is most relevant to which field of study?

(A) Geography

(B) Archaeology

(C) Physics

(D) Geology

Jawaban : (D) Geology

7. “ Curtains” can also be called …

(A) column.

(B) draperies.

(C) stalagmites.

(D) rims.

Jawaban : (B) draperies.

8. The word speleothem comes from which language?

(A) Latin

(B) French

(C) Greek

(D) English

Jawaban : (C) Greek

9. Stalagmites are formed by …

(A) drops of water which enter the cave through cracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water which seeps through the cave floor.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (A) drops of water which enter the cave through cracks in the ceiling.

10. Which speleothem hangs from the ceiling of a cave?

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Columns

(D) Rimstones

Jawaban : (B) Stalactites
