4. Complete with "so" or "such" to the following sentences!1. Professor Setiawan is _____ a stern taskmaster that lazy students won't take his class.2. The restaurant patron at the table near us was _____ belligerent that we all felt embarrassed, especially when he swept everything off the table and demanded his money back.3. The intricate metal lacework on the Eiffel Tower in Paris was _____ complicated that the structure took more than two and a half years to complete.4. Akbar and Anjar are _____ hard-working carpenters that I'm sure they'll make a success of their new business.5. The children had _____ much fun at the carnival that they begged to go again.6. I feel like I have _____ little energy that I wonder if I'm getting sick.​


Jawaban:

﻿Adverb & Determiner:

1. Professor Setiawan is SUCH a stern taskmaster that lazy students won't take his class.

2. The restaurant patron at the table near us was SO belligerent that we all felt embarrassed, especially when he swept everything off the table and demanded his money back.

3. The intricate metal lacework on the Eiffel Tower in Paris was SO complicated that the structure took more than two and a half years to complete.

4. Akbar and Anjar are SUCH hard-working carpenters that I'm sure they'll make a success of their new business.

5. The children had SO much fun at the carnival that they begged to go again.

6. I feel like I have SO little energy that I wonder if I'm getting sick.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Adverb dan Determiner (kata keterangan dan penentu) yaitu So dan Such dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kita memakai Adverb (kata keterangan):

So + Adjective + a/an + Singular Noun

Kita memakai Determiner (penentu):

such + a/an + adjective + Singular Noun

such + adjective + Uncountable Noun

Semoga membantu ya.

Jawaban:

PLO termasuk dalam kategori subjek hukum internasional belligerent. Berdasarkan paparan diatas, PLO menyatukan semua organisasi, memiliki wadah serta pemimpin serta mendapatkan status di Sidang Umum PBB maka tidak lagi merupakan insurgent namun telah menjadi belligerent.

Penjelasan:

Insurgent merupakan pemberontakan atau gerakan separatis dengan menggunakan senjata dengan tujuan untuk mencapai keberhasilan suatu kelompok tertentu. Sedangkan belligerent merupakan tahapan lanjutan dari insurgent yang sudah mencapai keteraturan layaknya suatu organisasi yang terstruktur dalam melakukan perlawanan.
