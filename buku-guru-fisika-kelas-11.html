FLUIDA STATIS
• tekanan

ρₐ = 10³ kg/m³
g = 10 m/s²
h = 10 m
76 cmHg = 10⁵ Pa
P = __?

tekanan hidrostatis
Pₕ = ρₐ g h
Pₕ = 10³ • 9,8 • 10
Pₕ = 9,8×10⁴ Pa
Pₕ = (9,8×10⁴ Pa) (76 cmHg / 10⁵ Pa)
Pₕ = 74,48 cmHg

tekanan total
P = P₀ + Pₕ
P = 76 + 74,48
P = 105,48 cmHg ✓
