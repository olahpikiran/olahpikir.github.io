Bathing at night are usually associated with the onset of rheumatism. Have you ever heard of this myth? We need to let you know that the actual bathing at night is not related to the onset of rheumatism.

Rheumatism is an inflammatory disease of the joints and muscles that can be suffered by all ages. The risk of someone getting rheumatism will increase with age. This is due to the depletion of the joint protective layer and thickening of lubricating oil over the age of 45 years. But it does not rule out the possibility of rheumatism also attacks young children, even babies.

Most people may believe the myth that bathing at night can cause rheumatism. In fact, so far there is no evidence to confirm that night baths will cause rheumatic disease. In principle a night bath or cold shower does not cause rheumatism. In patients with rheumatism, cold water baths can indeed make stiff muscles or spasm. This condition usually makes the joint suppressed so that it triggers pain.

Rheumatism can actually be caused by excessive consumption of foods such as innards, seafood, and alcoholic beverages that can trigger purines or ingredients to be converted into gout.

As for the actual 'bathing at night' is fine, because it is not directly related to the occurrence of an illness. But it's good to pay attention to the following:

1. Body condition (fit / healthy or sick)

2. The air temperature at that time (whether it's very cold or not)

3. Circumstances / temperature of the water (We recommend that the water is not too cold / the temperature is too extreme)

If the body's condition is not healthy, the air temperature at that time is cold, or the water for bathing is very cold, then you should not bathe at night with cold water. Because it can cause the body to become cold and this can cause discomfort, sometimes immunity can go down and easily hurt (such as a cold).

The temperature of the water that can be accepted by the body is a maximum of five degrees Celsius with body temperature. Normal body temperature is 36 degrees Celsius, so that the water temperature that can be received between 29-30 degrees Celsius.
