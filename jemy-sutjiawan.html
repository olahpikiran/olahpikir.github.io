Jawaban:

a. Loni (drive) her car to school last week

(+) Loni drove her car to school last week

(-) Loni did not drive her car to school last week

(?) Did Loni drive her car to school last week?

b. Jemy (fall) on the floor last night

(+) Jemy fell on the floor last night

(-) Jemy didn't fall on the floor last night

(?) Did Jemy fall on the floor last night?

Penjelasan:

Simple Past Tense

Fungsi : Menyatakan suatu perbuatan atau peristiwa yang terjadi pada waktu lampau.

Kalimat Nominal

(+) S + was/were + O + Adv

(-) S + was/were + not + O + Adv

(?) Was/were + S + O + Adv

Kalimat Verbal

(+) S + V2 + O + Adv

(-) S + did + not + V1 + O + Adv

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + Adv

• Was digunakan untuk subjek : I, He, She, It.

• Were digunakan untuk subjek : You, We, They.

• Did merupakan kata kerja bantu (auxilary verb) untuk kalimat negatif

• Did selalu diikuti oleh V1

• Keterangan waktu : yesterday, last..., dan ...ago.
