10. i would rather.....(read) english book than maths book


read aja ga usah ditambahin ing lagi soal nya dia pake subject (I)Answer: 

When you say about preference, you can use more than one expression. Here they are:
- prefer (S + prefer + noun 1 + to + noun 2)
  example: I prefer tea to coffee.
             (S + prefer to + V1 + than  + V1)
  example: I prefer to play football than watch badminton.
             (S + would prefer + noun 1 + rather than + noun 2)
  example: I would prefer read classic novel rather than read modern chick lit stories.
- would rather ( S + would rather + V1 + than + V1)
  example: I would rather play chess than sing.
 
So, the answer for the exercise above is 'read'.
I would rather read English book than Math book.

Kelas: SMA

Kategori: expression of interest and preference

24. you (read) a book(+)(-)(?)


(+) you read a book
(–) you don't read a book
(?) do you read a book?You (read) a book.

Karena kalimat di atas tidak ada bentuk waktu yang bisa membedakan kalimat simple present atau simple past, maka disini say menjawab menggunakan kedua tenses ini.


SIMPLE PRESENT
(+) Subject + V1 (s/es) + Complement
(-) Subject + Do/Does + Not + V1 + Complement
(?) Do/Does + Subject + V1 + complement.

(+) You read a book
(-) You do not read a book.
(?) Do you read a book?


SIMPLE PAST
(+) Subject + V2 + Complement
(-) Subject + did + not + V1 + Complement
(?) Did + subject + V1 + complement

(+) You read a book.
(-) You did not read a book.
(?) Did you read a book?
