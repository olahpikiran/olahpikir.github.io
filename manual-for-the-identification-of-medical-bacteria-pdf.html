Rumus

U_7

cari a terlebih dahulu

400 = a. 2^4-1

400 = a. 2^3

400 = a . 8

a = 50

selanjutnya kita cari U_7

U_7 = a. r^n-1

U_7 = 50. 2^7-1

U_7 = 3200

29. a. How many bacteria are in the culture at the end of the first hour?b. How many bacteria are in the culture at the end of 6 hours?​

a. Banyak bakteri setelah 1 jam

10 x 2^1 = 10 x 2 = 10

b. Banyak bakteri setelah 6 jam

10 x 2^6 = 10 x 64 = 640
