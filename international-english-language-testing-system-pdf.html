1. English is International language, ... ?​


English is International language, isn't it?

PEMBAHASANQuestion Tag

Merupakan pertanyaan pendek yang ditambahkan di akhir kalimat. Kalau di bahasa Indonesia, seperti kata “kan” atau “ya kan?”

Rule:

Positive sentence ➡ Negative question tag.

Negative sentence ➡ Positive question tag.

Kalau kalimat pernyataannya positif, maka diikuti question tag negatif. Pun sebaliknya, kalau kalimatnya negatif maka question tag-nya positif.

Contoh:

This bag is theirs, isn't it?

Tas ini milik mereka, kan?

Sundanese is not International language, is it?

Bahasa Sunda bukan bahasa internasional, kan?

You go to school everyday, don't you?

Kau berangkat sekolah setiap hari, ya kan?

John has finished his homework, hasn't he?

John sudah mengerjakan PR-nya, kan?

~ Based on Question

English is International language, ... ?

➡ English is International language, isn't it?

Bahasa Inggris adalah bahasa internasional, kan?

28. what is meant by testing language production and language recognition?


1.In psycholinguistics, language production is the production of spoken or written language. It describes all of the stages between having a concept, and translating that concept into linguistic form.

2.language recognition refers to the auto- matic process through which we determine or verify the identity of the language spoken in a speech sample. We study a computational framework that allows such a decision to be made in a quantitative manner.
