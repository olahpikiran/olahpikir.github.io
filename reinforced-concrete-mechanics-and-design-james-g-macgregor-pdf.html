2. contoh kata abstract Noun and concrete​


Jawaban:

Contoh Kata Abstract Noun :

1. Envy : Iri

2. Goodness : Kebaikan

3. Truth : Kenyataan

Contoh Kata Concrete :

1. Friend : Teman

2. Teacher : Guru

3. Television : Televisi

Answers and Explanation:

'Abstract noun' adalah semua benda yang tidak bisa dilihat oleh mata. Maksudnya, benda itu tidak bisa dirasakan oleh panca indra kita sama sekali.

Examples:

Knowledge (ilmu pengetahuan)Affection (kasih sayang)Friendship (persahabatan)Happiness (kesenangan/kegembiraan)Etc.

Sedangkan 'concrete noun' adalah semua benda yang benar-benar bisa dirasakan oleh kelima indra kita. Bisa dilihat, diraba, dirasa, disentuh, dan/atau didengar.

Examples:

Book (buku)Teacher (guru)Music (musik)Voice (suara)Wind (angin)Light (cahaya)Etc.

8. What is abstract noun, concrete noun and give examples each other


Jawaban:

Abstract noun- A noun denoting an idea, quality, or state rather than a concrete object, example: truth, danger, happiness.

Concrete noun- A concrete noun is a noun that you can see, hear, smell, touch, and taste, example: book is a concrete noun. You can touch it, see it, and maybe even smell it.

13. (+)... (-)... (?) Is hea mechanics


(+) He is a mechanic

(-) He is not a mechanic

(?) is he a mechanic?

Catatan:

Kalimat tersebut adalah Present Tense bentuk Nominal Sentence:

(+) S + To Be (is/am/are) + O

(-) S + To Be (is/am/are) + Not + O

(?) To Be (is/am/are) + S + O?

Catatan Subject + To Be

I = am

You, we, they = are

He, she, it = is
