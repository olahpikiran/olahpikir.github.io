1. Fading effect adalah :


Jawaban:

Umumnya koefisien gesek kampas rem akan berkurang seiring dengan kenaikan temperatur kampas dan teromol atau cakram. Proses ini disebut fading. Dalam kondisi sehari-hari, gejala ini kerap timbul jika pengemudi terus menerus menginjak rem di jalan menurun. Rem akan terasa kurang pakem karena semakin panas.

Ellen was a young girl who loved to play in the snow with her pet dog, Butch. One morning, after breakfast, she put on her snowsuit, boots, mittens, and hood and went outside to play. Her mother laughed and said, "Goodness, Jack has certainly painted your nose!"

Ellen was confused, but her brother Paul explained that Jack Frost was the one who painted the pictures on the windows every winter morning. He would come at night with an icicle for a paintbrush and snowflakes for his paintbox. He would breathe on the window to create a misty gray and then quickly draw his pictures. Ellen was fascinated and asked Paul to show her how it was done. Paul blew on the window and created a misty gray, and then drew a comical face with his finger. "See?" he said, "Jack Frost paints the pictures with his finger."

Ellen was disappointed that she didn't have an icicle paintbrush like Jack Frost, but she was happy to know that she could still create her own pictures with her finger. From that day on, she and Paul would spend their mornings creating their own winter wonderland on the windows.
