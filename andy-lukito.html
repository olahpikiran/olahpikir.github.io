Journey of Life and Career Ronny Lukito
Ronny's teenage years in Bandung are full of simplicity and hard work, his parents have a small shop specializing in handbags, since 1976 when Ronny was seated at STM, the shop started producing his own handbags with the trademark Butterfly taken from the name of a Chinese sewing machine which they use, Ronny helps the material buy and also after so He delivers his merchandise to the customer. Before leaving for school, Ronny sells milk, after school he becomes a mechanic in a motorcycle repair shop.

In 1979, Ronny wanted to continue his higher education at Itenas, but seeing his parents unable to pay for his lectures grew his wishes. Then he helps his parents to grow their family business. Ronny begins to put his family's bag of bags into the Sun, though he gets a few orders and continues to develop the business. With a capital of less than 1 million, Ronny bought 2 sewing machines, sewing equipment and some materials to make bags. In 1983-1984, Ronny began to produce his bag with the help of an employee named Mang Uwon and he also wanted to be able to put the product into Matahari mall, the application to enter the bag product is always rejected until finally the 13th filing is accepted with the selling price of the bag not up to 300 thousand.

Ronny diligently toured the area to search for his bag product retailer partner to open up new markets. Ronny at the time did not really understand the world of business and marketing, until finally he decided to use the services of consultants. Ronny also attended financial management courses, studied management knowledge, followed seminars, and read books.

In 1984, Ronny bought an additional 600 m2 of production for the production, in 1986 Ronny purchased 6000 m2 of land again. After marrying in 1986, he hired marketing professionals. With a full struggle tirelessly eventually become a successful entrepreneur Ronny and now not only the bag that he produces but the gloves of mobile phones, wallets and many others.

Ronny Lukito And His 4 Owned Companies
The company owned by Ronny Lukito, B & B Inc., has 4 subsidiaries:
1. Eksonindo Multi Product Industry, this company owns backpack and casual bag with Exsport trademark.
2. Eigerindo Multi Product Industry, his company produces products that are oriented to adventure and nature activities with Eiger trademark.
3. Eksonindo Multi Product Industry Senajaya, The company produces products under the Bodypack trademark to reach students to executives. Initially the concept of this trademark is Street Gear but then changed the concept to Digital Lifestyle.
4. Persada Abadi.

In 1998 Eiger who had not owned a shop and just a rented house with 2 tailors now Eiger has owned a factory in Soreang, Bandung with about 800 tailors.
