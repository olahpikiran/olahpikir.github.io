Kalimat pasif atau passive voice digunakan untuk menunjukkan ketertarikan pada seseorang atau objek yang dikenai tindakan dan bukan seseorang atau objek yang melakukan tindakan. Jadi, hal atau orang yang terpenting akan menjadi subjek kalimat.

Rumus:

1. Simple Present Tense

S + V1 + O menjadi O + to be (am, is, are) + V3 + by + S.

2. Simple Past Tense

S + V2 + O menjadi O + to be (was, were) + V3 + by + S.

3. Simple Future Tense

S + will + V1 + O menjadi O + will be + V3 + by + S.

4. Present Continuous Tense

S + to be (am, is, are) + V1-ing + O menjadi O + to be (am, is, are) + being + V3 + by + S.

5. Past Continuous Tense

S + to be (was, were) + V1-ing + O menjadi O + to be (was, were) + being + V3 + by + S.

6. Present Perfect Tense

S + have/has + V3 + O menjadi O + have/has + been + V3 + by + S.

ExercisesChange the following sentences into negative,interrogative,and/or positive ones.1.(+) ismail wake us happy both in the world and in the hereafter.(-)..………….......…............….(?).......................................?yes,No,2.(+) your ritual ablution will become invalid after you break wind.(-).....................................(?)....................................?yes,No,3.(+)........................................(-)......…................................(?) will pilgrimage change our faith and character better?yes,No,4.(+) i am going to leave this islamic boarding house next week.(-).....................................(?)....................................?yes,No,5.(+)...................................(-)......................................(?) shall we have fasting next month?tolong bantu,sekalian penjelasan menggunakan rumus​


1. Ismail will wake us happy both in the world and in the hereafter.

(-) Ismail will not wake us happy both in the world and in the hereafter. (?) Will Ismail wake us happy both in the world and in the hereafter?

Yes, he(Ismail) will.

No, he will not.

2. Your ritual ablution will become invalid after you break wind.

(-)Your ritual ablution will not become invalid after you break wind.(?) Will your ritual ablution become invalid after you break wind?

Yes, it will.

3. Pilgrimage will change our faith and character better.

(-) Pilgrimage will change our faith and character better. (?) Will pilgrimage change our faith and character better?

Yes, it will.

4. I am going to leave this islamic boarding house next week.

(-) I am not going to leave this islamic boarding house next week.(?) Am I going to leave this islamic boarding house next week?

5. We shall have fasting next month.

(-) We shall not have fasting next month. (?) Shall we have fasting next month?

Yes, we shall.

No, we shan't.

EXPLICATIONFuture Simple

Digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang akan terjadi di masa depan.

Form :

will :

(+) S + will + V1(-) S + will + not + V1(?) Will + S + V1 + ?

➡ Yes, S + will.

➡ No, S + will not/won't.

be going to :

(+) S + to be(is/am/are) + going to + V1(-) S + to be(is/am/are) + not + going to + V1(?) To be(is/am/are) + S + going to + V1 + ?

➡ Yes, S + to be(is/am/are).

➡ No, S + to be(is/am/are) not.

shall :

(+) S + shall + V1(-) S + shall + not + V1(?) Shall + S + V1

➡ Yes, S + shall.

➡ No, S + shall not/shan't.

Note :

will digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang belum terencana. be going to digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang sudah terencana. shall hanya bisa digunakan untuk subject I dan we. Details :
