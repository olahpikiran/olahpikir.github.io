Simple Past Tense

Simple Past tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi dan berakhir di masa lampau. Keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan pada simple past tense adalah:

1. Yesterday

2. Last night

3. Last week

4. Last month

5. Last year

6. Three days ago

7. Five weeks ago

8. two months ago

9. four years ago

10. this morning

11. this afternoon

12. dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan pada simple past tense adalah:

(+) S + V2

(-) S + did not + V1

(?) Did + S + V1?

18. terjemahan buku paket bahasa inggris kelas 8 halaman 77​


There is/There are

The roads are very busy right now, because there are many vehicles. There are also many pedestrians. There is a little discipline on the road, but there are very few policemen around.  

The translation:

Jalan-jalan saat ini sangat sibuk, karena banyak kendaraan. Ada juga banyak pejalan kaki. Ada sedikit kedisiplinan di jalan, tetapi hanya ada sedikit polisi di sekitar.

In big cities, there are many traffic jams everywhere. There are many kinds of vehicles: cars, big buses, minibuses, trucks, bicycles, motorcycles and becaks. Luckily there are not many carts now.

The translation:

Di kota-kota besar banyak terjadi kemacetan dimana-mana. Jenis kendaraannya bermacam-macam: mobil, bus besar, minibus, truk, sepeda, sepeda motor dan becak. Untungnya tidak banyak gerobak sekarang.

PEMBAHASAN:

There is/ there are digunakan untuk menunjukkan keberadaan benda dan orang. Kita menggunakan "there is" dengan kata benda tunggal (singular nouns) dan kita menggunakan "there are" dengan kata benda jamak (plural nouns).

Perhatikan contoh berikut ini!

There is a pen in my bag. There are pens in my bag. There is one child on the bed. There are several children on the bed

Untuk membentuk kalimat negative dengan dengan "there is" dan "there are" dengan cara menempatkan "not" sesudah to be (is atau are). Biasanya digunakan bentuk singkatan "isn't" dan "aren't".

Perhatikan contoh berikut ini!

There is not a pen in my bag. There are not pens in my bag. There is not one child on the bed. There are not several children on the bed

Untuk membentuk kalimat interrogative dengan dengan "there is" dan "there are" dengan cara menempatkan to be (is atau are) sebelum "there".  

Perhatikan contoh berikut ini!

Is here a pen in my bag. Are there pens in my bag. Is there one child on the bed. Are there several children on the bed

Jawaban:

Instruksi soal diatas tidak jelas. Namun berdasarkan gambar, kemungkinan besar ini soal tentang penulisan narasi. Berikut ini adalah jawaban untuk nomor 2-5 tentang kelanjutan narasi nomor 1.

2. Mrs. Wayan apparently had fallen from the stairs to her basement in her house when she was trying to go down to get her laundry. We thought there was her son in her house to take care of her.

3. When her cry for a help was louder, we decided to check on her. So, we went into her house instantly and called out her name. She heard our shout and found that Mrs.Wayan was in the basement.

4. She couldn't stand up nor sit down. What's worse, she broke her leg and was out of breath. My wife phoned the hospital and her son as soon as possible.

5. A few minutes later, her son arrived after we brought Mrs.Wayan to her bedroom. Yet, she laid in there unconsciously. Unfortunately, when the ambulance took her to the hospital, she passed away. Apparently she broke her spine as well and her chance to be in recovery was not much. Feeling regretful, her son could only weep in front of us. It was the gloomiest night of ours.

Pembahasan:

In writing narrative, there are several stuff to keep in mind, such as:

- The tense, which should be in past tense.

- The plot, which can be written in linear or using flashback and shall be made cohesively so that it's easier for the reader to understand.

- The characters, which need to be decided about the number of them and who's the major and the minor.

- The ending.

- The connectors, which is used to connect events in the story.

- if possible, the hidden message in the story or also called 'the moral value'.

Writing narrative can be made into informal one as its main function to entertain as well as inform the reader about something.

30. Terjemahan soal bahasa Inggris buku paket kelas 8 halaman 136


1.) Balkis: "Menurutku bunga sebenarnya lebih indah daripada bunga kertas. Bunga kertas lebih indah daripada bunga plastik. Jadi, bunga sebenarnya adalah yang paling indah dari semua bunga."

2.) Putu: "Heni tingginya 165 cm. Susi tingginya 158 cm. Tinggi Ita 150 cm. Heni lebih tinggi dari Susi. Susi lebih tinggi daripada Ita. Jadi. Heni yang tertinggi, dan Ita yang terpendek."

3.) Tasia: "Menurut kakak saya, sebuah bus besar lebih nyaman daripada sebuah bus kecil. Bus kecil lebih nyaman daripada bis mikro. Jadi, sebuah bus besar adalah yang paling nyaman dari semuanya."

4.) Hendrik: "Ular lebih panjang daripada belut. Belut lebih panjang daripada cacing tanah. Jadi, ular yang terpanjang, dan cacing tanah yang terpendek."

5.) Bayu: " Ayahku selalu berkata bahwa buah segar lebih enak daripada jus buah. Jus buah lebih enak daripada buah kaleng. Jadi, buah segar yang terenak dari semuanya."

6.) Hasim: "Rumah sakit berjarak 3 km dari sini, bank berjarak 4 km dari sini, dan kantor pos berjarak 5 km dari sini. Rumah sakit lebih dekat daripada bank, bank lebih dekat daripada kantor pos. Jadi, rumah sakit adalah yang terdekat, dan kantor pos yang terjauh."
