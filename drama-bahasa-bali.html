( Bahasa Inggris )

Kecak dance is a traditional art, a type of dance drama that is typical of Bali. The dance depicts wayang stories, especially the Ramayana story, which is performed with the art of movement and dance. This Kecak dance is one of the most famous traditional arts in Bali. Apart from being a cultural heritage, this Kecak dance is also one of the attractions for tourists who come there.

( Additional )

According to historical sources, the Kecak dance was created in 1930 by Balinese artists named Wayan Limbak and Walter Spies, a painter from Germany. This dance is inspired by the sanghyang ritual and parts of the Ramayana story. The sanghyang ritual itself is a dance tradition where the dancer is in an unconscious state and communicates with God or the spirits of the ancestors, then conveys his hopes to the community. The name of the Kecak dance itself is taken from the word "cak..cak..cak" which is often shouted by the members who surround the dancers.
